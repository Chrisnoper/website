What is Pramuka?

Pramuka is the Indonesian Scout Movement, a national youth organization that fosters character, leadership, and outdoor survival skills. It is based on the principles of Scouting founded by Baden Powell, but uniquely adapted to Indonesian culture and values. Pramuka teaches discipline, independence, cooperation, and patriotism through various outdoor activities, training, and community service.

In Indonesia, Scout is an important part of school education. Every student is encouraged to join and participate in camps, flag ceremonies, and competitions that challenge both mental and physical abilities.

I am proud to be a Scout graduate from SMK 5 Tangerang Selatan, where I achieved the rank of Penegak Laksana. I also studied at SMP 20 Tangerang Selatan, where my Scout journey first began.
